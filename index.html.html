User Journey: Referral to Discharge

Bromley Y — Digital Transformation Project — Draft, April 2026

This document maps the target user journey for young people accessing Bromley Y services, from initial referral through to discharge. It covers the two core delivery streams — Mental Health Support Teams (MHST) and Mental Health and Emotional Wellbeing Services (MHEWS) — and the shared intake pathway through iSPA.

Click any step to view further detail. Steps read left to right within each phase; where steps sit in the same column, they happen at the same stage. System tags on each card show which platforms are involved.
